Natural Networking

Bexley Linkup (or 'Blink'), was created by a group of small business owners with a mission to create friendly, informal networking events for sole traders, start-ups, entrepreneurs and established businesses based in the London Borough of Bexley and beyond.

The Bexley Linkup group meets on the second Wednesday of every month at a convenient location, usually at a local pub or restaurant. Our events are free to attend and open to all.

Bexley Linkup's logo consists of three people within an open circle, representing the connections that we create between businesses, whilst being open to all businesses, large or small, whether within or outside of Bexley. The main (vertical) logo features the complete group's name and the tagline (Linking Bexley Businesses), whereas the square logo features only the symbol and the abbreviated form of the name (Blink).

Sybiltec designed and developed a simple, one-page website to inform visitors of the upcoming events. We chose four colours for the colour palette; the navy blue of Bexley, and the three other colours representing the diverse range of businesses that we cater to.

The website includes Book Now buttons which link to the Ticket Tailor website, where visitors can register themselves onto the meetings. The web page also features a newsletter signup form, created with Mailchimp, allowing visitors to subscribe to our monthly newsletters to be informed about upcoming events. Our future plans include adding a gallery to showcase photos taken during the events.

Sybiltec Web Design is proud to be a supporter of Bexley Linkup. Special thanks also to babelMonkey, CNT Associates, Graham Baker Photography and Rubylox for making this project possible.
