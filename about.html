About Sybil

Thank you for visiting our website. My name is Sybil Mayard. I founded Sybiltec in 2020 with a mission to make web design and development services more accessible and affordable to all, especially small businesses. Four years later, I'm proud to say that we've worked with several SMEs from various industry sectors to help them achieve their digital goals. Our main services include websites for small businesses, e-commerce websites, and online learning platforms.

Current clients include the Krach Institute for Tech Diplomacy at Purdue, which involves designing and building an online portal for the new Tech Diplomacy Academy project which has just launched. I am also pleased to be working together with CNT Associates to deliver the Bexley Talking Business programme in association with the London Borough of Bexley and fully funded by the UK Shared Prosperity Fund. More information can be found on the Bexley Web Design page.

I graduated in November 2019 from the University of Kent with a Distinction in MSc Computer Science. Previous roles include:

  • QA Engineer at Esme Learning and Riff Analytics
  • Web Manager at Esme Learning
  • Junior Analyst at Capacitas

I am also the founder of VidCareer. It's a start-up idea which began during my Masters course, and is an online platform for connecting small businesses with candidates through virtual career events, saving time and money. For more information about VidCareer and my other projects, please explore the Projects page.

The Team

As a business owner, you are probably aware that having a beautiful website is only one aspect of your business. Have you thought about photography, brand planning, copywriting, social media, data protection, or raising funds? We are committed to delivering the highest quality services to our customers in London, Kent and beyond, therefore, I work alongside other professionals in related fields to provide much more than just a web design service. My team of experts can help you make your project a breeze. By partnering with the most knowledgeable and experienced individuals, you can rest assured that your project is in good hands. We take a collaborative approach and will work together with you to elevate your online presence and help your business achieve its potential.
