My Experience

Hi there! My name is Sybil Mayard. I graduated in November 2019 from the University of Kent with a Distinction in MSc Computer Science. My goal is to establish myself as a freelance IT consultant and help businesses develop their online presence. Services I can offer include website development, e-commerce, Google Analytics and SEO (search engine optimisation). For more information on how I can help you, please see the Services page.

My current project is called VidCareer. It's a start-up idea which began during my Masters course, and is an online platform for connecting small businesses with candidates through virtual career events, saving time and money.

For more information about VidCareer and my other projects, please explore the Projects menu.

Previously, I worked for a Capacitas, a London-based IT consultancy specialising in load testing and performance testing of clients' websites. My role as a Junior Analyst involved scripting the user journey with Apache JMeter, running the load tests, analysing the results, then compiling a technical report for the client of the issues to be investigated.

Other tasks included: improving the SEO of our company website by identifying and fixing broken links, optimising content, and managing blog posts via HubSpot. I also analysed Google Analytics data of a major retailer to research relationships between conversion rate and page load time.
