Hi, I'm Sybil: your human partner in a digital world

Technology is rapidly transforming the way we do business, empowering us to operate faster and more efficiently than ever before. However, keeping up with the speed of technology can be overwhelming. With the vast number of tools at your disposal, how do you decide on the best tools to utilise? And how will you find the time to set up said tools?

In an increasingly digital and AI-driven world, it can be hard to find the right partner for your business. That's where I come in. I understand your frustrations, and I'm here to take care of your technology so you can focus on what matters in your business.

I pride myself on offering a personalised service, so working with me, you'll benefit from a one-to-one, jargon-free service, where the human element is a top priority. So, if you have tech troubles, digital dilemmas or website worries, get in touch with me today!

Background

I'm a freelance web designer and digital consultant. I founded Sybiltec in 2020 with a mission to make digital services accessible to all small businesses.

For the past five years, I've been designing and developing websites for small business owners, helping them to maximise their online presence. I'm proud to have worked with clients from various industry sectors, including tech, consulting, creative, e-commerce and construction. My clients are based primarily in the UK, but I have successfully worked with clients in France and the USA.

In my spare time I enjoy building websites like this one 🙂

Not only do I build websites, but I also support business owners with related areas including email marketing, automation, CRM, project management, cyber security basics, and more.
