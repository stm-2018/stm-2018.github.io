At the Centre of Our Community

The New Haw Community Centre is located in New Haw, Surrey. The previous website suffered from an outdated design and a nonfunctional booking system.

The Solution

Sybiltec worked with the Community Centre to create a new design for the website. Using the logo as the basis for the colour palette, we used the red of the "New Haw Community Centre" text as the primary colour and the font (Harabara) for the headings. For the body text, we chose a sans-serif font (Mulish), for its ease of readability.

The homepage was completely redesigned, placing a large, colourful image in the background of the hero section. We ensured to include the most important sections on the homepage which were not previously present, i.e. the two halls, Why Choose Us, image gallery, additional facilities and testimonials.

An Easier Way to Book Facilities

To fix the booking system, we set up the LemonBooking app and connected the Book Now button to it, so users can now easily find an available date and time to book the required facilities without having to send emails back and forth to the Bookings team.

The Result

The Community Centre now enjoys a website with a fresh, modern design and a user-friendly booking system.
