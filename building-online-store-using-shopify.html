Free Webinar: Building an Online Store Using Shopify

Date published: 13 February 2024

Are you a business owner who is planning to sell your products and/or services online? Then consider building an online store with Shopify, one of the world's most popular e-commerce platforms.

Attend our FREE webinar on Tuesday 12th March 2024 at 11am for an overview of how to set up an e-commerce store with Shopify. No prior knowledge of the platform is needed.

To register now and secure your place on the webinar, click the button below:

Register Now

The webinar will cover:

  • Introduction to e-commerce
  • Features and benefits of Shopify
  • Setting up a Shopify account
  • Managing your online store
  • Growing a new or existing business
  • Marketing your e-commerce business

This webinar is delivered as part of our free Business Support programme, funded by the London Borough of Bexley's Talking Business initiative. It is open to all SMEs and sole traders in Bexley, and to others who may be interested.

Speakers

This webinar will be delivered by two presenters who are experienced in the management and technical aspects pertinent to online stores and Shopify:

Gary Parker, Managing Director of CNT Associates, a London-based business support/fundraising consultancy, with over 20 years' experience of fundraising.

Sybil Mayard, founder of Sybiltec, helping small businesses to improve their online presence through better web design.
