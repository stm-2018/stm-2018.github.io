TagNav

Helping you navigate your social life

Finding Your Way

TagNav is an app concept that was developed by two students (Asha Burathoki, Olcay Kilincci) and I, during our time at the University of Kent. It is very common for students to experience feelings of depression and isolation, especially when moving to campus for the first time. To help ease the transition, we designed TagNav: an app which helps students connect with like-minded individuals by helping them to find interesting and relevant events on- and off-campus, without the added noise associated with existing social media platforms.

Our idea was shortlisted as finalists at several business competitions in 2018, including the Business Start-Up Journey and the Big Ideas Competition.

To showcase our app idea, we created a microsite where you can find out more about the project.
