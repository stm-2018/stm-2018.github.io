Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Why do I need a website?

Every day there are thousands of searches being carried out on search engines by people looking for information on products and services. Having a good online presence is one of the best ways to get new customers and so one of the first things you'd want to do to improve your online presence is to create a website.

There are many reasons why you may need a website. If you are a startup you may be trying to secure funding, and you need a proof of concept. Or if you run a medium-sized business you may need a larger website to showcase your services, clients, case studies, testimonials, portfolio of work and so on.

But websites are not just for startups or larger businesses - even if you are a sole trader or self-employed, and you run a coaching or mentoring business for example, then having a personal website is crucial for you too.

Which platform is best for my needs?

WordPress is the most flexible platform, so if you need a website that will adapt as your business grows, then I would recommend WordPress. However, there is a learning curve associated with it, so if your time is limited and you want to get started more quickly, then Wix would be more suitable as it's user-friendly for those who are managing their first website. Then finally we have Shopify: if you run an e-commerce business then Shopify is the best choice as it includes everything you need to get started with selling your products or services online.

How can I improve the design of my homepage?

Here are a few tips that you can apply to improve the design of your homepage:

  1. If a visitor is viewing your website for the first time, would it be clear to them what your business does within the first 3 seconds? If not, make some changes!
  2. Put the most important information "above the fold" (this is what the visitor sees when they first open your homepage, without scrolling down)
  3. Check whether your website displays beautifully on desktop, mobile and tablet devices
  4. Remove any large photos or videos that may be slowing down the website
  5. Place a strong call-to-action on the homepage so that visitors know what to do. This can be a button like "Contact Us", "Get a Quote" or "Buy Now"
  6. Don't use too many colours and fonts.

Which Shopify plan is best for me?

If you are just beginning your e-commerce business, then I would recommend choosing the Basic Shopify plan which provides everything you need to start selling your products or services online. When you feel that you are ready to take your business to the next level, you can upgrade to the Shopify plan, which offers advanced features and an enhanced e-commerce experience.
