Frequently asked questions

See the list below for the answers to your frequently asked questions (FAQs).

How much do you charge?
This would very much depend on the work to be carried out. Please get in touch to arrange an initial consultation, after which I can provide a detailed quote for your requirements.

How long does the work take?
The time it takes to complete the work would depend on what is involved. A 5-page website could be completed in as little as 4-6 weeks depending on how quickly I receive the required information. In order for the work to be completed as soon as possible, please provide the required information promptly.

Which platforms do you develop on?
I develop websites on WordPress, Wix, Shopify and Squarespace.

Would we need to meet in person?
Until further notice, all meetings will be via Zoom (or an alternative video conferencing platform of your choice).

What is the process for developing a website?
The first step would be to arrange a free consultation to discuss your requirements. After this initial meeting, I would provide you with detailed quote. If you are happy to proceed, I will send you an agreement. Once the agreement has been signed by both of us, I will proceed with the work.

Can I see examples of your previous work?
Of course, please see my Clients page for examples of past work I have carried out for clients.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science from the University of Kent.
