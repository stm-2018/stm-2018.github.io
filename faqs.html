FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge?

This would very much depend on the requirements of the project. Please get in touch to arrange an initial consultation, after which I can provide a detailed quote for you.

How long does the work take?

The time it takes to complete the work would depend on the complexity of the project. A 5-page website could be completed in as little as 4 weeks depending on how quickly I receive the required information. In order for the work to be completed as soon as possible, please provide the required information promptly. An estimated timeframe for completion of the project can be discussed during our initial call.

What do I need to supply you with?

  • Copy and content for each page
  • Branding guidelines
  • Company logo
  • Any partner logos
  • High quality images (if you don't have your own images, I can provide you with links to free stock photos)
  • Links to social media profiles
  • Links to videos
  • Access to your domain and hosting (if applicable)
  • Examples of websites to use for inspiration

Preferably, I would like to work with documents submitted via Google Drive - I can provide you with a link to a shared folder. Alternatively, please send me the content by email.

Which platforms do you develop on?

New websites will be developed on Wix, WordPress or Shopify. If you have an existing website on an alternative platform, e.g. BigCommerce or Squarespace, I can provide support for these too.

What is the process for developing a website?

The first step would be to arrange a free consultation to discuss your requirements. After this initial meeting, I would provide you with a detailed quote. If you are happy to proceed, I will send you an agreement. Once the agreement has been signed by both of us, I will proceed with the work.

If you need a complete website to be developed, I will begin by producing the designs. After you have reviewed the designs and there are no further changes, I will start building the site in your chosen website builder. I will ask for feedback via email on a regular basis to ensure that the site is built according to your specifications. When you are happy with the website and I am satisfied that sufficient testing has been carried out, we will agree on a date to launch the site. I will then transfer ownership of the website to you.

If you have an existing website and you require my maintenance services, I will request access to the platform. Once I receive the required permissions I will begin with the work. I will send you regular updates via email so that you are always aware of the status of the project.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Of course, please see my Clients and Design page for examples of past work I have carried out for clients.

What are your qualifications?

I hold an MSc in Computer Science from the University of Kent.

Are you an SEO expert?

No - SEO is a separate field in which I am not an expert. I can perform basic SEO improvements to your website (e.g. page titles, meta descriptions and alt tags) but if you require more advanced improvements I would suggest to work with a suitably qualified SEO professional.

Are you a copywriter?

I do not offer a copywriting service; please provide me with all of the copy you would like to add to the website. However, I am happy to proofread any text supplied in English. If you need a multilingual website, please also provide me with the translated copy.
