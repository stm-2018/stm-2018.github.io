Webinar: Regulators Are Watching Your Website: Creatively Manage Website Compliance

Date and time: Wed, 12 Apr 2023 11:00 - 12:00 BST

Location: Online

Introduction:

Website compliance matters. Regulators are watching you!

This webinar will provide you with a general overview of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and data protection requirements for websites. We'll cover the basics on how to improve the design and data protection compliance of your website, including:

  • Websites and data protection considerations
  • Quick win tips to avoid fines or regulatory action
  • Where to go for clear data protection and website design advice
  • The rules in relation to cookies, marketing, and newsletters

About This Event:

This webinar will help you to improve your website quickly and efficiently. We will cover the key data protection and design considerations for your website. It is recommended for small business owners who:

  • have a website
  • want to improve their website
  • want to know more about data protection and websites
  • want one-to-one guidance from experts.

You will meet a data protection expert; a website designer and you will have an opportunity to network with other like-minded business owners too.

What is Included?

At these sessions you will get clear, practical advice on the key elements and practical components of data protection rules in relation to:

  • website design and structure
  • privacy notices
  • complaints
  • breaches

You will get the most from the session if you have already thought about how you would like to boost the design and compliance of your website. The challenges we discuss will help you to produce a strong action plan.

As you will be meeting a data protection expert, why not get on-the-spot answers to your data protection and website design questions.

Workshop: Funding and Opportunities for E-commerce

Date and time: Tue, 25 Apr 2023 11:00 - 12:30 BST

Location: The British Library, 96 Euston Road, London NW1 2DB

We are pleased to be running this workshop in association with the British Library - Business Support Programming.

Are you looking for funding and opportunities to boost your business?

Join our workshop to gain insights into various sources of funding and potential grants, loans and other opportunities that can help you grow your business.

In this workshop you will learn about e-commerce platforms and gain awareness and knowledge on how to identify areas for development. You will also be equipped with the necessary tools to identify potential sources of funding and learn more about funding opportunities.

Key Learning Points:

  • Discover various sources of funding, including grants, loans, consultancy support, and 'support in kind'
  • Understand e-commerce platforms and other online services that can empower and enable your business
  • Learn about specific funding streams
  • Explore funding opportunities from local sources, government agencies, regeneration programs, and other public bodies
  • Gain insights into charitable Trust Funding and other community funding programmes
  • Access lists of key websites and other relevant information that can support your funding strategy

The two speakers are:

Gary Parker, Managing Director of CNT Associates, a London-based business support/fundraising consultancy, with over 20 years' experience of business support.

Sybil Mayard, founder of Sybiltec, helping small businesses to improve their online presence through better web design.
