Webinars and Workshops to Support Your Business

I have partnered with CNT Associates to produce a series of events delivered via Zoom, to help you start and grow your business. To find out more and to register, click the relevant link below.

Shopify - An Introduction to Using it to Build an Online Store

When: Tuesday 17th August 2021 at 11:00am-12:00pm BST

Where: Zoom (online)

Cost: Free

This free presentation will be presented by two experienced facilitators, Gary Parker BA (Hons) Social Science - who will present the overview and introduction, and Sybil Mayard MSc Computer Science - who will facilitate the main technical aspects of the presentation. Both will answer questions after the presentation.

This workshop will teach you the basics of:

  • How to set up a Shopify account
  • How to set up an online store
  • How to grow a new or existing business
  • Hints and tips to make the most of your online business
  • Information, advice and guidance on the next steps
