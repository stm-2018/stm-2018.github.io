An Introduction to Building a Business Using Shopify

When: Thursday 21st January 2021 at 2pm GMT

Where: Zoom (online)

Cost: Free

This free event will be hosted by two experienced facilitators, Gary Parker, BA (Hons) Social Science - who will present the overview and introduction and Sybil Mayard, MSc Computer Science - who will facilitate the main technical aspects. Both will answer questions after the presentation.

This workshop will teach you the basics of:

  • How to set up a Shopify account
  • How to set up an online store

  • Hints and tips to make the most of your online business
