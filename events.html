Webinars and Workshops to Support Your Business

I have partnered with CNT Associates to produce a series of events delivered via Zoom, to help you start and grow your business. To find out more and to register, click the relevant button below.

Setting Up an Online Store For Your Business Using Shopify

When: Thursday 5th May 2022 at 10:00am-11:00am BST

Where: Zoom (online)

Cost: Free

This free presentation will be delivered by two experienced facilitators, Gary Parker, BA (Hons) Social Science, who will present the overview and introduction, and Sybil Mayard, MSc Computer Science, who will facilitate the main technical aspects. Both will answer questions after the presentation.

This workshop will teach you the basics of:

  • How to set up a Shopify account
  • How to set up an online store
  • How to grow a new or existing business
  • Hints and tips to make the most of your online business
  • Information, advice and guidance on the next steps
