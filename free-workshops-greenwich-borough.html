Helping Your Business Thrive

We are pleased to be offering a new series of free workshops to businesses and organisations based in Greenwich. The workshops are delivered both online and in-person.

Our workshops cover a range of topics, including websites, e-commerce, funding, digital marketing and more. They are beginner-friendly, so no prior experience of these topics is required to attend.

Preference is given to Greenwich-based organisations, however, all are welcome to attend.

RBG Generative Business Support Programme

These workshops are delivered in partnership with CNT Associates and the Royal Borough of Greenwich. The series is part of the RBG Generative Business Support Programme, fully-funded by the Government's UKSPF (UK Shared Prosperity Fund). The programme provides help to non-profits, CICs and charities in Greenwich with various areas including business planning, website reviews and digital marketing.

During the workshops, we will provide you with the opportunity to find out more about the programme and how to register if you are eligible.

If you have any questions about these workshops or the business support programme, please contact Gary Parker on info@cntassociates.com.

Support for non-Greenwich Businesses

We also offer free support to businesses and organisations in other London boroughs. If your business is registered and/or trading in the London Borough of Bexley, please see our Web Design for Bexley Small Businesses page.
