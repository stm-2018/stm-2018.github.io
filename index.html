Your Website Worries - Sorted!

Website problems - we all have them. As a small business owner, it's likely that you've faced one or more of these issues at some stage during the development of your online presence. Here is a list of recent website problems that my clients have complained about; if you can relate to any of these, get in touch with me today to discuss the best solution for you.

Contact Me
  • My web designer is too distant and extremely slow to respond. Even the smallest requests take ages to implement. I need someone with good communication who I can trust to get the job done quickly.
  • I run my business on a very limited online presence. I need a website to expand my online presence beyond Facebook and Instagram.
  • Our website hasn't been updated for over 10 years. It is built upon a very outdated technology. Upgrading to WordPress would allow us to build a website with a bespoke design that reflects our brand.
  • Our editing capabilities are very restricted. Any major changes to our website require the services of a web developer, and we are charged for every edit. We need a platform that allows us to make the changes ourselves.
  • Our eBay shop is becoming increasingly expensive to run and we are bound by their terms and conditions. A system like Shopify would be more cost effective and allow us full control over the look and feel of our store, as well as how we manage the customer experience.
