Bexley Talking Business

Date published: 14 December 2023 | by Sybil Mayard

Need Business Support?

Are you a small business owner or sole trader based in the London Borough of Bexley? Are you seeking support with your business?

Running a business can be challenging, but you don't have to do it alone.

The new Talking Business - Business Support programme has recently been launched, and is designed to help create 'good growth' for small businesses across the borough. The programme is funded by the Government's UK Shared Prosperity Fund (UKSPF).

I am delighted to announce that I have partnered with CNT Associates to deliver the Talking Business - Business Support programme, which helps small business owners through a variety of different formats including webinars, advice sessions (both 1-2-1s and in groups), online resources and networking. We cover a range of areas, including financing, developing an e-commerce store, digital marketing, website design and support for women-led businesses.

As a participant on this free programme, you will receive:

  • Business support - 6 hours
  • Help with completing templates
  • 10 webinars
  • Access to an online portal for useful information and resources

The programme will be running until early 2025.

Eligibility

To be eligible for this programme:

  • Your business must be registered and/or trading in Bexley
  • Businesses must be an SME (0-249 employees)
